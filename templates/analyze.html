Dynamical control Workshop

17 March to 21 March 2014

Perspectives in Dynamically involved material, with each speaker presented his/her perspective on the subject. Amongst others, the topics of the talks included pattern generation and nonlinear control, geometry of collective control and dynamics, numerical issues in nonlinear control, control of mechanical systems.

Objectives
  • Establish international linkages & cooperative partnership in the thematic area of complex & nonlinear dynamical systems.
  • Upgradation of research knowledge and research competence.
  • International exchange of credits, faculty & students.
  • Joint publications, research proposals.
Resource Persons
  • Roger W. Brockett ,Harvard School of Engineering and Applied Sciences
  • D. E. Chang, University of Waterloo, Canada
  • Prof. A. J. Krener, UC DAVIS University of California
  • Prof. Tudor Ratiu, Geometrical Analysis EPFL, Switzerland
